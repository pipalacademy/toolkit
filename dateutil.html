Date Utilities¶

toolkit.dateutil.relative_date(date_string, reference_date)¶

Get the date relative to the truncated date of the given date.

Parameters:

  • date_string – format is (date|week|month)(+|-)(n)

  • reference_date – the date to find relative date on

toolkit.dateutil.truncate_date(date, timescale: str)¶

Truncates a date to nearest week or month.

Parameters:

  • date – the date to truncate

  • timescale – one of day/week/month or daily/weekly/monthly

Returns:

truncated date
